Three generations of drive: building a legacy in the GTA.

Sharpline Home Comfort was built on the foundation of hard work, resilience, and family. Our story begins with our father, Shelton, who immigrated to Canada from Sri Lanka in 2000 with a vision to create a better future for his family. Like many great Canadian stories, the company started humbly—through long nights, weekends, and an unwavering commitment to doing honest, high-quality work.

What began as a small side effort steadily grew through dedication and word-of-mouth into a trusted name across the Greater Toronto Area. For over two decades, the GTA has been our home, and we take immense pride in the relationships we’ve built and the thousands of homeowners and businesses we’ve served with their electrical and HVAC needs.

Today, that same foundation continues to drive us forward. With over 25 years of hands-on field experience, we are expanding into the next generation of home comfort and energy systems. By combining traditional trade craftsmanship with modern technologies such as high-efficiency HVAC, electrification, and clean energy solutions, we are helping bridge the gap between proven expertise and the future of energy independence across Canada.
